Get Started

See how you can get the EDC up and running in your environment in a matter of minutes.

01

One-Click Deploy

Once you have deployed your database, download our one-click installation wizard to get the EDC up and running in a matter of minutes.

02

Add A Connector

Simply fill in a few fields to allow the EDC to generate a connection to your database. We currently support MS SQL, MySQL, and Oracle to allow your team to choose how they wish to get work done.

03

Connect Your Application To The EDC

Instead of connecting to your database directly in your project, add a connection to the EDC in the web config file of your project to begin routing all database traffic through the system.

04

Begin Working

Now that the connection has been established, your application immediately begins to benefit from our features such as authentication, logging, and permissioning which allows you to start building new features straight away.
